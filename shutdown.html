The Shutdown

On June 15, 2022, after roughly 26 years, Internet Explorer met it's end. It continued to have updates from August of 1995 all the way to its last update, Internet Explorer 11, that was releases in October of 2013. This shutdown forced users who wanted to stay with Microsoft to use the newer browser called Microsoft Edge.
